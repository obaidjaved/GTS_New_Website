OUR STORY

Driven by Passion, Focused on Results

Globotech Solutions was founded with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between complex technology and real business growth. What started as a small team of passionate developers has grown into a premier full-service digital agency. We pride ourselves on the fact that we never outsource—every line of code, pixel of design, and deployment script is handled directly by our in-house experts in the USA.

We work closely with our clients as strategic partners, ensuring every project is delivered on-time, within budget, and built to the highest industry standards of performance, security, and scalability. This makes us a trusted companion in your journey to digital growth.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects ship within 4–10 weeks. AI integrations 4–6 weeks, Shopify/WordPress 3–5 weeks, mobile apps 8–12 weeks. Fixed timelines are scoped upfront so you always know the delivery date.

Do you work with startups or only established businesses?

Both. Funded startups, bootstrapped founders, SMBs, enterprise. The common thread: clients who are serious about quality and know what they want to build.

What does your communication process look like?

Dedicated Slack channel, bi-weekly demos, async updates every 2–3 days, live milestone dashboard. You're never in the dark.

Can you integrate AI into an existing system?

Yes — this is where we shine most. We've integrated LLMs and AI workflows into dozens of existing systems across legacy and modern stacks.

Is post-launch support included?

30 days post-launch support at no extra cost. Monthly retainers available after that. 98% of clients continue working with us beyond the initial project.
