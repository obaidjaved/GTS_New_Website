FAQ

Common Questions,
Answered.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent requests, call us directly — we have team members available across USA and Pakistan time zones.

What information should I include in my message?

The more detail the better — your goals, timeline, approximate budget, and what you've already built (if anything). Even a rough idea is enough to get started. We'll ask the right questions to scope the project properly.

Do you sign NDAs before the first call?

Absolutely. We're happy to sign an NDA before any discovery call. Your idea and business details are completely confidential from the first conversation.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects ship within 4–10 weeks. AI integrations 4–6 weeks, Shopify/WordPress 3–5 weeks, mobile apps 8–12 weeks. Fixed timelines are scoped upfront so you always know the delivery date.

Is post-launch support included?

30 days post-launch support at no extra cost. Monthly retainers available after that. 98% of clients continue working with us beyond the initial project.
